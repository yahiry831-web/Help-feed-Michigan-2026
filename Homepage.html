Food Access Michigan

Food Access Michigan is a 2026 research initiative focused on addressing food insecurity across communities in Michigan. This project highlights key data, real-world impacts, and potential solutions to improve access to nutritious food for individuals and families in need.

Through awareness, community engagement, and data-driven insights, this initiative aims to support long-term solutions and inspire action toward a more food-secure future.
